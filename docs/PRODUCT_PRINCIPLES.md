# EMBUR Product Principles

## 1.
Return Time.

Every feature must give owners more time.

## 2.
Reduce Stress.

If it creates anxiety,
remove it.

## 3.
One Focus.

Every screen has one primary action.

## 4.
Guide.

Don't overwhelm.

Tell owners what matters next.

## 5.
Recover.

Recover opportunities before they disappear.

## 6.
Invisible Intelligence.

Never show AI.

Simply be smart.

## 7.
Confidence.

The owner should always feel:

"My business is under control."

## 8.
Beautiful Simplicity.

Less.

Always less.
